GolfTracker Support

GolfTracker is a golf tracking app for iPhone and Apple Watch. For help, feedback, bug reports, or data requests, email jared.gantt+golftracker@gmail.com.

Common requests

  • Feature requests and feedback — email with a description and, if relevant, the app version (Settings → About).
  • Delete your data — open Settings → Delete my data in the app. See the Privacy Policy for details.
